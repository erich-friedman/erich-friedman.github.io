Problem of the Month (March 2010)

Packing circles, squares, or triangles inside each other has been well-studied. See my Packing Center for details. This month we investigate packing two of these shapes (with sides or diameter 1) inside one of them. Can you fill in the missing entries, or improve the existing entries, in the tables below?
