Crossing Flashlights

There is a person with a flashlight located at every digit in these puzzles. Draw a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal light ray coming from each person, which will not leave the grid or enter a square with a digit. Each light ray should cross a number of other light rays equal to the digit it comes from. No two people are shining their flashlights at each other. The first puzzle is solved as an example.
