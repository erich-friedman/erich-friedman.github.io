Holiday Puzzles 2023

1. PRODUCT COMPARISONS
Place a positive integer in each empty square so that two multiplication equations are true. Digits vertically adjacent will be within 1 of each other. Vertical <, >, or = indicate whether they are equal or which one is bigger.

2. MORE CHRISTMAS EMOJI SONGS

3. EASY OR KNOT

Position 13 non-overlapping cubes to form a trefoil knot.

 

4. FLAGS

Put each of the international alphabet flags shown above into one of the 26 white squares shown below, either right side up or with a 45o clockwise rotation. When two flags share part of an edge, they must be the same color there. When a flag has a corner on the edge of another flag, the color of the CORNER of the first flag must match the color of the CENTER of the second flag.
