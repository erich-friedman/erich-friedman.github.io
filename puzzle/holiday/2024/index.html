Holiday Puzzles 2024

1. TIME FOR A CHANGE
In my pocket, I have some nickels, dimes, and quarters, a different number of each totaling less than a dollar. Strangely, if my nickels were dimes, my dimes were quarters, and my quarters were nickels, I'd have the same amount of money. How many of each coin do I have?

2. STRAIGHT AND CURVY
Put a digit 0, 1, Z, 3, 4, S, 6, 7, or 8 (Yes, the 2 has been straightened, and the 5 has been curled) in each empty square so that straight digits (1, Z, 4, 7) count the number of (horizontally, vertically and diagonally) adjacent straight digits, and curvy digits (0, 3, S, 6, 8) count the number adjacent curvy digits. One digit has been placed in each puzzle to help you out.

3. TOWERS OF POWERS

Find two different solutions to the equation abc = def, where a, b, c, d, e, and f are 6 different digits other than 0 or 1.

4. HAPPY NEW YEAR!

Fill in the empty circles with numbers. Each circle should contain either the product or the absolute difference of the numbers in the two circles directly above it.
