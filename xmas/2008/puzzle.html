Christmas Puzzles 2008

1. HO-HO-HO

Fill in letters in the squares
to make common English
words. Letters in like colored
squares will be the same.

2. TREE PRODUCTS

Place a different positive integer
in each circle. Each number
will be the product of the digits
of the numbers pointing to it.

3. HAVING A BALL

Cut the ornament into 24 equal
shapes. Four of the shapes have
already been cut to start you off.

4. THAT TIME OF YEAR

Place the digits 1-9, each
used once, into the circles
to make the equation true.

5. WEIRD WREATH

What is so unusual about this
wreath of L-shaped regions?
